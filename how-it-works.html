How It Works

A clear, unhurried process — so you always know what's happening next and why.

1

Reach Out

Call, email, or fill out our contact form to tell us a little about your situation — the home, the timeline, and what kind of help you're looking for. There's no obligation and no pressure.

Usually takes 5–10 minutes
2

Free In-Home Consultation

We visit the home (or connect by video, if that's easier) to see the scope of work, listen to your priorities, and answer your questions. This is where we get to know your family's needs.

Scheduled at your convenience
3

Personalized Plan & Quote

We put together a clear, written plan that outlines the services involved, an estimated timeline, and transparent pricing — so there are no surprises along the way.

Delivered within 1–2 business days
4

Sorting & Organizing

Our team works through the home methodically, sorting belongings into keep, donate, sell, and dispose categories, and setting aside keepsakes and important documents for your review.

Paced to your comfort level
5

Clearing & Relocation

We coordinate donation drop-offs, disposal, and moving logistics — packing and delivering keepsakes to family, arranging storage, or scheduling movers as needed.

We handle the vendors and logistics
6

Home Preparation

Once the home is cleared, we arrange cleaning, minor repairs, and light staging so it's ready to sell, rent, or pass on to its next chapter.

Coordinated with your realtor if needed
7

Final Walkthrough

We walk through the home together to confirm everything is complete, answer any last questions, and hand over keys, documents, or inventory records.

Your satisfaction, confirmed in person
Good to Know

Flexible by design

Not every family needs all seven steps. Some just need help with one afternoon of sorting; others need the full process from first call to final walkthrough. We scale to fit.
