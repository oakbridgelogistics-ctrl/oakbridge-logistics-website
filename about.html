About OakBridge Logistics

A local, family-minded team built around one idea: nobody should have to face a home full of memories alone.

Our Story

Built to bridge one chapter to the next

OakBridge Logistics was founded in Henderson, Nevada, to fill a gap we kept seeing: families dealing with the loss of a loved one, a downsizing move, or a sudden life change were left to manage an overwhelming, unfamiliar process almost entirely on their own — often while still grieving.

Our name reflects our purpose. Like the oak that stands over a bridge, we aim to be steady and grounding, helping families cross from one chapter of life into the next with support they can lean on.

Today, we work with families throughout Henderson and the greater Las Vegas Valley, offering everything from a single afternoon of sorting to full-service estate transitions.
